Everything you need to know about the science and practice of Silensia.

  • What is Hypnotherapy?

    A state of focused attention where the mind is highly responsive to positive suggestion. It is not mind control; you are always in charge.

  • Is it Scientifically Proven?

    Yes. Clinical studies show that guided neuromodulation can rewire neural pathways, reducing cortisol and enhancing GABA production.

  • Frequency of Practice

    Consistency is key. We recommend one 15-20 minute session daily for at least 21 days to see measurable neuroplastic changes.

  • Safe for Everyone?

    While safe for most, we recommend consulting a physician if you have a history of severe mental health conditions like epilepsy.
